# XCUIDevice.Button.action

The device’s action button.

iOSiPadOSMac CatalystwatchOSXcode 16.3+

``` source
case action
```

## Discussion

The button you use for specific actions in workout apps, available in watchOS 9 and later on Apple Watch Ultra. You can use hasHardwareButton(_:) to check for its availability from your test.
