Resolution of the shape.

iOS 18.0+iPadOS 18.0+Mac Catalyst 18.0+macOS 15.0+visionOS 2.0+

var boundaryResolution: MeshResource.ShapeExtrusionOptions.CurveStrokeResolution

## Discussion

The default value is MeshResource.ShapeExtrusionOptions.CurveStrokeResolution.uniformSegmentsPerSpan(segmentCount:) with a segment count of 6.
