# forearmWrist

An anchor location at the wrist end of forearm on the back of the hand.

visionOS 2.0+

``` source
static let forearmWrist: AnchoringComponent.Target.HandLocation.HandJoint
```

## Discussion

Content anchored this way has its positive y-axis pointing in normal direction of the dorsum of hand and its negative z-axis towards the side where the thumb is.
