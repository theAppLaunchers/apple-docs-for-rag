# Gender Constants

Constants that indicate the gender of the individual represented by avoice.

## Overview

These constants are used in the `gender` field of the VoiceDescription structure.

## Topics

### Constants

var kNeuter: Int16

Neuter voice.

var kMale: Int16

Male voice.

var kFemale: Int16

Female voice.
