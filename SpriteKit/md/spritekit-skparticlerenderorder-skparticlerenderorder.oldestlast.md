# SKParticleRenderOrder.oldestLast

The particles are rendered from newest to oldest. This is the default value.

iOS 9.0+iPadOS 9.0+Mac Catalyst 13.1+macOS 10.11+tvOS 9.0+visionOS 1.0+watchOS 2.0+

case oldestLast

case oldestFirst
