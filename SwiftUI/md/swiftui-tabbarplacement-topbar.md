# topBar

Top bar of a tab view.

iOS 18.0+iPadOS 18.0+Mac Catalyst 18.0+macOS 15.0+tvOS 18.0+visionOS 2.0+watchOS 11.0+

## Discussion

A tab bar can display as the top bar in tabBarOnly on iPad and macOS, or sidebarAdaptable on iPad.
