# AudioUnitParameterUnit.cents

A logarithmic unit of measure for a musical interval between two notes.

iOSiPadOSMac CatalystmacOStvOSvisionOS

``` source
case cents
```

## Discussion

Relative musical pitch in cents, where 1,200 cents are equal to one octave. 100 cents are equal to one semitone. See also the AudioUnitParameterUnit.absoluteCents property.
