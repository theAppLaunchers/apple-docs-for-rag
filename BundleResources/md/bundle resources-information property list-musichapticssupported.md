# MusicHapticsSupported

A Boolean value that indicates to the system that your app supports the Music Haptics feature.

iOS 18.0+iPadOS 18.0+

## Details 

Name  
Supports Music Haptics

Type  

boolean

## Discussion

To notify the system that your app supports the Music Haptics feature, add MusicHapticsSupported with a value of `YES` to your appâ€™s `Info.plist`. When you add this key to your `Info.plist`, your app appears in the list of apps that support Music Haptics in Settings. For more information, read Music Haptics.
