# Creatives

Create and manage ad creatives within your organization.

## Overview

You assign Creative objects to your Custom Product Pages. You can assign one Creative per custom product page per organization. After assigning your creative, the next step is to Create an Ad object to assign to an ad group.

## Topics

### Creative Endpoints

Create a Creative

Creates a creative object within an organization.

Find Creatives

Finds creatives within an organization.

Get a Creative

Fetches a creative by identifier.

Get All Creatives

Fetches all creatives within an organization.
