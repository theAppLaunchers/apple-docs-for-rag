# Background Elements

Control background images and media items that play in the background.

## Overview

Compound background elements are used to display images behind the rest of the template elements and to play audio in the background of a client-server app page.

## Topics

### Background Elements

audio

Plays background audio for a full-page template when the template is the top-most document in the navigation stack.

background

Displays an image or plays audio in the background of a channel app page.
