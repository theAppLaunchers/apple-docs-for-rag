# useUnixLineSeparators

Treat only `\n` as a line separator (otherwise, all standard line separators are used).

iOS 2.0+iPadOS 2.0+Mac Catalyst 13.0+macOS 10.0+tvOS 9.0+visionOS 1.0+watchOS 2.0+
